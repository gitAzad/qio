Privacy Policy for Qio

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Qio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application uses your information.

Camera Permission

Our application requires access to your device's camera to function properly. This access is strictly used for:

  • Scanning QR codes and Barcodes in real-time.

We do not transmit, store, or share any image data or camera feed on our servers. All processing of camera data happens locally on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information (PII) from our users. The scan history is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
